CARE INSTRUCTIONS
=================

General Care Tips:
- Read care labels on all garments
- Sort laundry by color and fabric type
- Use appropriate water temperature
- Hang or fold items properly to prevent wrinkles
- Store seasonal items in breathable containers

Fabric-Specific Care:
- Cotton: Machine wash warm, tumble dry low
- Denim: Wash inside out, cold water, hang dry
- Wool: Hand wash or dry clean only
- Silk: Dry clean or hand wash gently
- Synthetic: Machine wash cold, air dry
